01 About

Background

I sit at the intersection of communications, product, growth, and community. I help companies explain what they do, launch what they build, and turn audiences into active users, customers, and partners.

With more than 20 years in communications, content, and management, I have built and led teams across gaming, Web3, media, and local news. My work spans the full operator stack: brand positioning, GTM planning, community growth, partnership development, event execution, and PR.

Since 2021, I have led communications and growth efforts tied to over $1B in combined transaction volume across blockchain gaming platforms. I have navigated multiple market cycles with a clear, solutions-first mindset and earned a reputation as a trusted operator who can align communities, partners, and teams to execute at a high level.

I build strong relationships with players, partners, media, and leadership teams. Whether coordinating a product launch, activating a partnership at a global conference, or building a content system from scratch, I focus on outcomes that move the business forward.
